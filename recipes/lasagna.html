Lasagna Recipe

World's Best Lasagna

A picture of the finished lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Italian Sasuage
  • Lean Ground Beef
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Two cans of tomato sauce.
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesen Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Dried Basil
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Black Seeds

Instructions

  1. Create a meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make a ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake the lasagna.
  6. Let the lasagna sit before serving.
