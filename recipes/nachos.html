Resturant-style Chicken Nachos

Resturant-style Chicken Nachos

A picture of the finished nachos.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of canola oil.
  • 6 green onions, sliced, white parts and tops seperated.
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 shredded, cooked, whole chicken breast.
  • Salt and pepper to taste.
  • Package of tortilla chips.
  • 8 ounces of shredded Monterey Jack cheese blend.
  • 1/2 Large tomato, diced.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Heat oil in 12 inch skill over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the white parts of green onions and garlic.
  4. Cook until the onions are tender. Make sure to continously stir the mixture.
  5. Mix in chicken ,salt and pepper. Toss skillet until mixture is well cooked.
  6. Stir in salsa.
  7. Place torilla chips on large baking sheet. Pour the skillet mixture on to chips.
  8. Top torilla chips with shredded cheese and tomatos.
  9. Bake in oven for 10 minutes.
  10. Remove baking sheet from over and top torilla chips with the tops of the green onions.
